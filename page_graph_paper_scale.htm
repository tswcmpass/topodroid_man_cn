Graph-paper scale

This dialog allows to set a factor in the computation of the scale of the graph-paper.

It displays a segment of (world) length 5 m at scale 1:100. Therefore the segment should be 5 cm long on the screen.
You can adjust the length of the segment with the "plus" and "minus" buttons.
Tap the "OK" button to confirm your choice, "cancel" to discard it.
