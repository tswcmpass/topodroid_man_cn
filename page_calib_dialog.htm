DistoX CALIBRATIONS

This dialog is opened from the Calibration button of the Device window.

Buttons:

  • new calibration
  • import a calibration from the CSV export

Underneath there is the list of the calibrations of the active device.

Open a calibration

Tap the calibration entry in the list to open it in the Calibration Info window.

New calibration opens the Calibration Info window, where you can enter the data for the new calibration, and create it. After it is saved to the database you can open it (ie, the Calibration Data window), download the calibration data, compute the calibration coefficients and upload them to the DistoX.
