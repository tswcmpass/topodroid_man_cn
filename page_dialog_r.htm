Android R dialog

Starting with Android-11, TopoDroid cannot access files in the usual way, as of a Google requirement.
The app private files (device database, drawing symbols, firmware files, etc.) are stored in the app private space, "Android/data/com.topodroid.TDX/files", where other apps do not have access.
This folder is deleted by the system when you uninstall the app.
The survey database and files are stored under "Documents/TDX/TopoDroid".
This folder is not deleted by the system when you uninstall the app, but TopoDroid cannot access it any more if you uninstall and reinstall the app.
In that case you have to rename "Documents/TDX" and cold-start TopoDroid so that it creates its new "TDX".
