3D VIEWER - 3D rose diagram

The dialog is opened from the menu 3D rose diagram of the 3D Viewer window.

It displays the 3D view of the directions of the survey legs.
The reference is made of a green line pointing East, a blue line pointing North, and a red line pointing upwards.

The direction of view is displayed on the top of the screen.
The inclination is restricted to negative values (ie, -90 means looking down and 0 means looking from a side).
